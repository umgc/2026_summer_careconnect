# Care Connect Account Deletion Request

## How to Submit a Deletion Request

To request deletion of your Care Connect account, please email:

**careconnect.support@gmail.com**

Include the following in your request:
- Full name
- Email address associated with your account
- Date of last use (if known)

You will receive a confirmation email within 5 business days acknowledging
your request. Account deactivation will be completed within 30 days of
verification.

## Important Notice Regarding Healthcare Data Retention

Care Connect is a healthcare coordination platform subject to the Health Insurance
Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and applicable state health data retention
laws. Federal regulations require that medical records, care tasks, health data, and
caregiving history be retained for a minimum of 6 years from the date of creation or
last use. This data cannot be permanently deleted upon request.

## What Happens When You Request Account Deletion

Upon receiving a verified account deletion request, Care Connect will:

- Deactivate your account and remove your login access within 30 days
- Remove or anonymize personally identifiable information where legally permissible
- Retain all health records, care tasks, caregiving history, and clinical data for
  the minimum required retention period in accordance with HIPAA
- Retain billing and subscription records in accordance with applicable financial
  regulations
- Notify you by email when your account has been deactivated

## Data That Will Be Removed

- Account credentials and login access
- Personal contact information where not attached to a retained health record
- Notification preferences and communication settings

## Data That Must Be Retained

- Medical and health records
- Care task history and caregiving activity logs
- Clinical notes and health tracking data
- Billing and subscription records
- Any data required by applicable federal or state law

All retained health and caregiving data is held for a minimum of 6 years
per HIPAA federal retention requirements.
