# Care Connect Support

## Contact Support

For help with Care Connect, please email:

**careconnect.support@gmail.com**

Include the following in your message:

* Full name
* Email address associated with your account
* A description of the issue or question

You will receive a response within 5 business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I create an account?**
Download CareConnect and tap Sign Up. You can register as a caregiver or patient.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions can be managed through your device's App Store or Google Play account settings.

**How do I delete my account?**
See our [Account Deletion Policy](https://github.com/umgc/2026_spring_careconnect/blob/main/ACCOUNT_DELETION.md).

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your email.

## Privacy & Data

CareConnect handles healthcare data in accordance with HIPAA regulations.
For questions about your data, contact careconnect.support@gmail.com.
